Welcome to Mason CC

Mason Competitive Cyber is the sole cybersecurity organization at GMU.

We don't require any prior experience from our members, but we try to accommodate all audiences of technical ability and some level of technicial proficiency (not in cybersecurity specifically) will dramatically benefit members. We cover both advanced and more fundamental topics in meetings and compete outside of meetings, coordinated via our chat.

Proud Sponsors

Thank you to the following companies for providing Mason CC with major support and/or donations:





The Crypsis Group, frequently referred to as Crypsis, is a company headquartered in McLean, VA that largely does digital forensics and incident response. Crypsis employs multiple CC members and is looking to hire more through their internship announced at the same time of the sponsorship. Crypsis also has spoken at CC, provided CC with pizza at a meeting, funded CC to a generous extent, and has had their work be inspiration for the Host Based Forensics talk and Love Your Logs talks. In addition to internal contacts, members could see their careers page for internship and job opportunities.



Battelle was our first ask for sponsorship and when we called they answered. With about 6 members in advanced training being actively employed by Battelle at the time of the sponsorship, our club's always had a distinct interest in Battelle as a company. With the support of Battelle, Mason CC will be able to pay for shirts, potentially CCDC registration, and more to further our unity as an organization and further legitamize our mission. In addition to internal contacts, members could see their careers page for internship and job opportunities. Active competitors may also be interested in the Battelle Cyber Challenge that prioritizes candidates.

In addition, thank you to GRIMM Co. for partnering with us to provide us a variety of challenges and a talk to keep our members entertained.
