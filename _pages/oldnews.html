Mason CC proud to accept sponsorship from Battelle

We are happy to announce we've accepted sponsorship from Battelle to further our education mission and compete in bigger and better ways. We've pledged to spend the money on shirts first, with plans to later spend it on CCDC registration pending we sort out a few things regarding CCDC's timeline. This will be recognized at our November 8th meeting and Battelle will be prominently displayed in most places Mason CC is as a result.

Mason CC participating in GRIMM HAX

Mason CC is happy to announce we expect to be participating in GRIMM's HAX program starting with their October challenge. Mason CC kicked off semester September 6th

Mason Competitive Cyber had their first meeting of the semester Wednesday, September 6th, in JC Room D (basic track) and SUB1 3B (advanced track) with a great deal of success.
